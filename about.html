About Me

Designing interactive experiences for science learning.

I’m passionate about the ways games, visualization, and interactive systems can make complex scientific ideas more engaging, accessible, and meaningful. I believe interactive media and digital tools are most effective when used alongside hands-on, collaborative, and experiential learning. Rather than replacing traditional learning environments, I am interested in how games, simulations, and visual media can support engagement, exploration, and deeper scientific understanding.

Across projects ranging from educational games to scientific illustration and public learning initiatives, I aim to design experiences that encourage curiosity and appreciation for the natural world.
