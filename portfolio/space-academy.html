Space Academy

Interactive Adobe Captivate learning module designed to teach stellar evolution and the life cycle of stars through guided learner interactions.

Adobe Captivate Instructional Design Interactive Media Science Education

Overview

Space Academy is an interactive science-learning module developed using Adobe Captivate. The module explores stellar evolution, star formation, and the life cycle of stars through guided interactions, multimedia content, and learner-driven exploration.

Design Challenges

A major challenge in developing this module was organizing complex astronomy concepts into a learning experience that remained engaging, visually approachable, and easy to navigate. The project required balancing instructional clarity, interaction pacing, and multimedia elements.
