ScanBright

Game-based radiology education platform focused on improving medical imaging interpretation and clinical learning experiences.

Medical Education Radiology Training Game-Based Learning

Overview

ScanBright is an interactive mobile learning platform specifically designed for radiology residents. Its primary goal is to support radiology education through simulations, learning games, and medical image interpretation activities. ScanBright draws inspiration from contemporary learning technologies, established educational frameworks, and current research in radiology education, beginning with the development of the first module, RadSwipe.

Design Challenges

A major challenge was translating complex radiology workflows and learning objectives into interactive experiences that remained engaging and accessible for learners. The project required balancing educational accuracy, usability, and meaningful gameplay interactions.

Reflection

This project strengthened my interest in simulations and game-based learning for medical and professional education, while highlighting the importance of close collaboration with subject matter experts.
